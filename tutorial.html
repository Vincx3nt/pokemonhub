📖 How to Play

Watch, Learn, and Become a PRO Pokémon Trainer

Phase 01
🎴

Draw & Setup

Shuffle your 60-card deck, draw 7 cards, and place your Active Pokémon. Don't forget to set aside 6 Prize Cards—your keys to victory!

Phase 02
⚔️

Power & Attack

Attach Energy to fuel powerful moves. Use Trainer cards to search your deck, evolve your Pokémon, and unleash massive damage on your opponent!

Phase 03
🏆

Claim Victory

Knock Out an opponent's Pokémon to take a Prize Card. Collect all 6, or clear their field of Pokémon, to be crowned the Champion!
